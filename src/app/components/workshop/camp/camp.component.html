Camp Kind is an engaging day camp designed to invite children of various ages into a world filled with activities, games, and community that center around promoting kindness in digital spaces. The camp embodies a creative, fun-filled environment where kids can learn about empathy, respect, and responsibility online, mirroring the values of The Kindness Code Foundation.

Activities (Ideas)


  • Digital Etiquette Workshops:
    Interactive sessions to teach children about respectful communication online.

  • Kindness Challenges:
    Daily challenges to promote positive behavior both online and offline.

  • Virtual Reality Empathy Experiences:
    Utilizing VR to help children understand the impact of their words and actions in a virtual space.

  • Cyber-Safety Training:
    Educating children about how to stay safe online.

  • Community Projects:
    Collaborative activities to promote teamwork and community service.

  • Creative Expression:
    Art and storytelling exercises to help children express what kindness means to them.


Age Groups


  • Group 1: Ages 5-7:
    Introduction to digital kindness with age-appropriate activities and games.

  • Group 2: Ages 8-10:
    More advanced workshops on digital etiquette, including beginner's safety online.

  • Group 3: Ages 11-13:
    In-depth cyber-safety training, challenges, and initial community engagement projects.

  • Group 4: Ages 14-17:
    Advanced workshops and real-world projects focusing on empathy, leadership, and community service.


Key Partnerships


  • Educational Institutions:
    Collaborate with schools to promote and host the camp, integrating it into extracurricular activities.

  • Technology Companies:
    Partner with tech firms to provide tools, platforms, and expertise, particularly for the VR experiences and coding exercises.

  • Community Organizations:
    Work with local nonprofits to align community service projects and extend the reach of Camp Kind.

  • Parent & Teacher Associations:
    Engage parents and teachers to ensure alignment with educational goals and parental expectations.


Desired Outcomes


  • Empathy and Compassion:
    Develop a sense of empathy and compassion towards others in digital spaces.

  • Safe Online Behavior:
    Understand and practice safe and respectful online behavior, recognizing potential threats.

  • Leadership Skills:
    Foster leadership and teamwork skills through collaborative projects and challenges.

  • Community Engagement:
    Encourage active participation in online communities with kindness and integrity, inspiring others to do the same.

  • Practical Application:
    Apply learned concepts in real-world scenarios, bridging the gap between theory and practice.



Camp Kind represents an exciting opportunity to extend the mission and values of The Kindness Code Foundation into a tangible, interactive experience for children. By crafting a program that balances fun and education, Camp Kind will be a beacon for kindness in the digital world, reflecting the loving spirit of Benny. It invites all who participate to make a positive change in themselves and the world around them.
