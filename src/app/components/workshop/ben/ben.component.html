Embark on a unique journey with "The Adventures of Sophie and Ben", where storytelling, technology, and education converge in a vibrant, compelling narrative. Meet Sophie, a young girl brimming with curiosity and Ben, her sentient robot companion. Their adventures span across exotic landscapes, wherein Ben strives to comprehend and emulate human emotions, guided by the insightful and empathetic Sophie.

Their encounters confront Ben with complex human emotions and characteristics. These scenarios, imbued with subtle instances of kindness and understanding, challenge Ben, pushing him beyond the boundaries of his programmed logic and algorithms.

The narrative doesn't just stop at the books. Each adventure forms the foundation of an interactive online workshop - "Ben's Brain". Here, children engage with the story's underlying concepts through hands-on visual coding activities. They explore and manipulate Ben's 'learnings', offering a tangible way to integrate logic, critical thinking, and creativity.

Who is this for?


Designed with grades K-2 in mind, the adventures of Sophie and Ben aim to engage and educate young minds of all ages. Parents, educators, and institutions who are seeking novel, exciting ways to introduce programming, logic, and critical thinking, paired with an understanding of human emotions, will find this project invaluable.

How will this benefit?


The narratives of Sophie and Ben extend beyond entertainment. Each adventure introduces a life lesson or a STEM concept, making it a fascinating educational journey. The online workshop serves as a practical extension of the learning, offering interactive activities that reinforce the lessons, resulting in:

  • STEM skills

  • Enhanced problem-solving abilities.

  • A more in-depth understanding of emotions.

  • An appreciation for empathy and kindness.


Target Audience


Although primarily aimed at children, we welcome partnerships with educational institutions, libraries, and ed-tech companies. Through expanding our reach via these channels, we can bring the adventures of Sophie and Ben to a global audience, thus amplifying our impact.
