The Adventures of Sophie and Ben


We are crafting an immersive, whimsical world of storytelling aimed at blending technology, education, and kindness into an engaging experience for children. The heart of this project revolves around the unique duo - Sophie, a young adventurous girl with an insatiable curiosity and a heart full of kindness, and Ben, her sentient robot companion who learns and adapts from their experiences and engagements with other creatures they cross paths with.

Their adventures take them across fascinating landscapes, introducing them to a plethora of remarkable characters, like Cammy the Chameleon who struggles with being unseen in a vibrant world, or Felix the firefly who struggles to forgive a mean nighttime creature that permenantly destroyed his light. As Sophie and Ben aid these characters, solving problems that require creativity, empathy, and critical thinking, they learn valuable lessons about kindness, adaptability, and understanding.

However, the innovation doesn't stop at storytelling. Each story forms the basis of an interactive online workshop. Ben, our robot, logs each adventure's data, learnings, and experiences into an online portal. Children can then dive into this platform, gaining hands-on coding experience while recreating and interacting with the solutions Ben and Sophie arrived at during their adventures. This innovative platform integrates narrative context into the world of coding, making learning a tangible, exciting journey rather than a static exercise.

Who is this for?


The project is primarily aimed at grades K-2, but its elements of fun, exploration, and learning can appeal to young minds of all ages. Parents, educators, and institutions looking for engaging and innovative methods to introduce children to the world of programming, logic and critical thinking mixed with discovering artifacts of kindness will also find value in this project.

How will this benefit?


The narrative of Sophie and Ben is designed to provoke curiosity and instill a love of learning. Each story embeds a kindness principle, a life lesson, or a STEM concept, making it an entertaining yet educational read. The accompanying online workshop complements the storytelling by offering hands-on, interactive activities that reinforce the lessons from the stories. Children will gain:

  • STEM skills, particularly programming and logic concepts.

  • Improved problem-solving abilities.

  • Social-emotional learning.

  • A greater understanding and appreciation of diversity, empathy, and kindness.


Target Audience


While the primary audience for this project is children, I am open to partnerships with educational institutions, libraries, and ed-tech companies. By scaling through these channels, we can bring the adventures of Sophie and Ben to more children around the world, thereby expanding our impact.
